No Limit Texas Hold'em (NLHE) is a poker game that involves betting and individual play, where the winner is determined by the ranks and combinations of their cards.

Gameplay

Players take turns dealing the cards and placing bets. Each player is dealt a hand of two cards, which they keep hidden from the other players. Community cards are dealt in the middle of the table. There are several rounds of betting, with players either folding, calling, or raising their bets. The final round is called the showdown, where the remaining players reveal their cards and the highest hand wins. Players with hands of equal rank split the pot.


Hand Rankings

The ranking of hands, from highest to lowest, is:

  • Royal Flush
  • Straight Flush
  • Four of a Kind
  • Full House
  • Flush
  • Straight
  • Three of a Kind
  • Two Pair
  • Pair
  • High Card
